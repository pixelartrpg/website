Game Mechanics - Overview

Secrets Of The Dreamers

Introduction:

In a world where the past and future intermingle through the minds of a chosen few, Dreamers are revered and feared. Born with the gift of prophetic dreams, they hold the key to secrets long forgotten and events yet to unfold. Dreamers can live for centuries, but their visions fade as they age, leaving only fragments of their once powerful insights.

Prologue:

The game opens with a cinematic scene: a shadowy council chamber where six figures argue vehemently around a circular table. The room is filled with an eerie light, flickering like a dying flame. The voices are heated, but one phrase stands out above the rest: "We cannot survive without the Seventh!"

Chapter 1: The Awakening

You start as an ordinary person in one of four starter disciplines:

  1. Cadet (Melee): A trainee in close combat, adept with swords, axes, and other melee weapons.
  2. Scout (Ranged): A nimble explorer skilled with bows, crossbows, and firearms.
  3. Acolyte (Healing Magic): A novice in the art of healing, capable of mending wounds and curing ailments.
  4. Apprentice (Damage Magic): An aspiring mage proficient in offensive spells and elemental magic.

Your journey begins with a vivid and unsettling dream about The Council of Seven. As you wake, you feel a strange connection to the dream, unlike anything you've experienced before.

Chapter 2: The Revelation

As you continue your journey, you encounter various challenges and characters that lead you to discover the existence of Dreamers. Through interactions and quests, you uncover that you are, in fact, one of the Dreamers. Your visions grow stronger and more frequent, revealing glimpses of both past and future events.

Chapter 3: The Search for the Seventh

Determined to understand your role, you set out to find the Seventh Dreamer, who vanished 70 years ago after dreaming of his own death. This quest takes you through ancient ruins, forgotten cities, and dangerous landscapes. Along the way, you uncover more about the history of Dreamers and the true nature of their powers.

Chapter 4: The Council's Dilemma

Upon finding the Seventh Dreamer, you learn that his disappearance was not out of fear, but a strategic move to protect a crucial secret. He reveals that the Council's arguments were about an impending catastrophe that only the combined power of all Dreamers could prevent. His dream of death was a metaphor for the potential end of the Dreamers' influence.

Chapter 5: Uniting the Dreamers

With the Seventh Dreamer's guidance, you work to reunite the Council. This involves resolving their conflicts, rebuilding trust, and preparing for the foretold catastrophe. Your visions become central to guiding their actions and decisions.

Chapter 6: The Final Prophecy

As the Council reconvenes, the full extent of the looming threat is revealed: a powerful force seeking to erase the existence of Dreamers and their knowledge. The game culminates in an epic battle where you and the Council must use your combined prophetic abilities to outmaneuver and defeat this enemy.

Chapter 7: The Eternal Dream

With the main story concluded and the Dreamers' legacy secured, your journey continues in "Secrets Of The Dreamers" through an open-ended chapter that offers limitless adventures. As a fully realized Dreamer, you are now free to explore the world, uncover hidden secrets, and face new challenges. This chapter revolves around the perpetual cycle of discovery, combat, and mastery, providing endless opportunities to hone your skills, gather resources, and make a lasting impact on the world..

 

Epilogue:

With the threat vanquished, the Dreamers' legacy is secured. The game ends with you standing among the Council, now a full member, contemplating the future and the dreams yet to come.

Character Progression System

Starting Disciplines:

  1. Cadet (Melee)
    • Primary Skills: Basic melee combat techniques, defensive stances, and physical conditioning.
  2. Scout (Ranged)
    • Primary Skills: Precision shooting, stealth tactics, and survival skills.
  3. Acolyte (Healing Magic)
    • Primary Skills: Basic healing spells, protective wards, and support magic.
  4. Apprentice (Damage Magic)
    • Primary Skills: Offensive spells, elemental manipulation, and arcane knowledge.

 

 

Chapter 1: The Awakening – Quests

Chapter 1 focuses on meeting the townspeople, exploring the area, meeting your class trainer, and culminating in a significant quest that reveals your deeper connection to the Dreamers.

Quest 1: Welcome to Ithoria Village

  • Objective: Introduce yourself to the village elder.
  • Description: Arrive in Ithoria Village and speak with Elder Galen to get an overview of the village and its people.
  • Reward: Basic equipment and a small amount of experience.

Quest 2: Friendly Faces

  • Objective: Meet key villagers and learn about their daily lives.
  • Description: Talk to the blacksmith, innkeeper, farmer, and healer to understand more about the village and gather helpful items.
  • Reward: Information about village life, small amounts of various resources, and a small amount of experience.

Quest 3: Exploring the Forest

  • Objective: Gather medicinal herbs in the nearby forest.
  • Description: Collect five medicinal herbs for the village healer in the forest.
  • Reward: Healing potions and a small amount of experience.

Quest 4: The Blacksmith’s Aid

  • Objective: Help the blacksmith gather iron ore.
  • Description: Collect ten pieces of ore from the nearby cave monsters.
  • Reward: Improved weapon and a small amount of experience.

Quest 5: Training with the Seventh

  • Objective: Report to your class trainer for initial training.
  • Description: Meet your class trainer and complete your initial training session. *** Spoiler: He is the 7th Dreamer. ***
  • Reward: Skill points and a small amount of experience.

Quest 6: The Night Watch

  • Objective: Assist the village guard with patrol duty.
  • Description: Patrol the village perimeter at night and report any suspicious activity.
  • Reward: Gold and a small amount of experience.

Quest 7: Visions of the Past

  • Objective: Undertake a quest unique to your class, involving seeing the past.
  • Description: Your class trainer sends you on a quest to retrieve an ancient artifact linked to your discipline. During the quest, you experience a vision of the past, revealing a crucial piece of history related to the Dreamers.
  • Reward: Significant experience, unique class-specific item, and basic understanding of your powers.

 

Chapter 2: The Revelation – Quests

These seven quests for Chapter 2 continue to build the narrative around your character’s discovery of their true nature as a Dreamer, deepening their understanding of their powers and the history of the Dreamers. Each quest is designed to provide more insight and progress the story meaningfully

Quest 1: Strange Visions

  • Objective: Investigate the recurring visions you have been experiencing.
  • Description: You are reluctant to tell anyone about your dreams. But your trainer keeps dropping hints that he may already know. He sends you on a seemingly pointless mission to investigate the old well.

Quest 2: Forgotten Lore

  • Objective: Research ancient texts in the village library.
  • Description: Find and study ancient texts that might explain the nature of Dreamers.
  • Reward: Lore knowledge and a small amount of experience.

Quest 3: The Hermit's Tale

  • Objective: Visit the hermit who lives on the outskirts of the village.
  • Description: Speak with the hermit who is said to know about the Dreamers.
  • Reward: Hermit's wisdom and a small amount of experience.

Quest 4: Echoes of the Past

  • Objective: Explore a haunted location where past events replay.
  • Description: Investigate a haunted site where echoes of past events might provide clues about the Dreamers.
  • Reward: Vision fragment and a small amount of experience.

Quest 5: The Historian's Insight

  • Objective: Meet with the historian of The Council of Seven.
  • Description: Gain an audience with the historian who can provide information about the Dreamers' past.
  • Reward: Historical documents and a small amount of experience.

Quest 6: The Seer's Stone

  • Objective: Retrieve a Seer's Stone from an ancient ruin.
  • Description: Journey to the ruins outside the village and recover the Seer's Stone to help understand your visions.
  • Reward: Seer's Stone and a small amount of experience.

Quest 7: The Truth Revealed

  • Objective: Undertake a significant quest that reveals the truth about your nature as a Dreamer.
  • Description: Follow the final vision to a secret location where you uncover a hidden truth about your abilities and the history of the Dreamers.
  • Reward: Major revelation, significant experience boost, and a unique skill upgrade.

 

Chapter 3: The Search for the Seventh - Quests

These quests for Chapter 3 will guide players through the challenging and intriguing journey of locating the Seventh Dreamer. Each quest builds on the previous one, creating a cohesive narrative that deepens the player's understanding of the Dreamers' history and their own role in the unfolding events.

Quest 1: Rumors of the Seventh

  • Objective: Gather information about the whereabouts of the Seventh Dreamer.
  • Description: Speak to villagers and travelers to collect rumors and clues about the Seventh Dreamer’s last known location.
  • Reward: Information about possible locations and a small amount of experience.

Quest 2: The Old Map

  • Objective: Find and decipher an old map that might lead to the Seventh Dreamer.
  • Description: Retrieve an old, partially damaged map from the village library and work with the historian to decipher it.
  • Reward: Map to a hidden location and a small amount of experience.

Quest 3: The Hidden Path

  • Objective: Follow the map to a secluded area.
  • Description: Travel to a remote part of the forest using the map to find the hidden path that might lead to the Seventh Dreamer.
  • Reward: Access to a new area and a small amount of experience.

Quest 4: The Abandoned Cabin

  • Objective: Investigate an abandoned cabin for clues.
  • Description: Explore the abandoned cabin in the secluded area for any signs or clues related to the Seventh Dreamer.
  • Reward: Clues about the Seventh Dreamer’s journey and a small amount of experience.

Quest 5: Echoes of the Past

  • Objective: Use your Dreamer abilities to experience a vision in the cabin.
  • Description: Use your prophetic abilities to trigger a vision in the cabin, revealing a key moment from the Seventh Dreamer’s past.
  • Reward: Vision revealing the next location to search and a small amount of experience.

Quest 6: The Mountain Pass

  • Objective: Navigate through a treacherous mountain pass.
  • Description: Follow the vision’s guidance to traverse a dangerous mountain pass leading to the Seventh Dreamer’s hiding place.
  • Reward: Mountain gear and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 7: The Truth

  • Objective: Find out the truth about the seventh dreamer.
  • Description: Reach the Seventh Dreamer’s sanctuary and find evidence that is in fact your trainer. Return to town and question him. Leading to you learning why he left the Council and what he knows about the impending catastrophe.
  • Reward: Major revelation about the Dreamers’ future, significant experience boost, and a unique skill or item.

Chapter 4: The Council's Dilemma – Quests

These quests for Chapter 4 focus on the critical task of unifying the Council, preparing for the impending threat, and ensuring that all members are aligned and ready to act. Each quest helps to build the narrative tension and sense of urgency, culminating in a united front ready to face the challenges ahead.

Quest 1: The Seventh's Warning

  • Objective: Return to the Council with the Seventh Dreamer's message.
  • Description: Travel back to the Council and relay the urgent warning from the Seventh Dreamer about the impending catastrophe.
  • Reward: Council's acknowledgment and a small amount of experience.
  • Story Element: The council did not believe the 7th, but after hearing that a new dreamer has been born (you) and that you are having the same visions. They are still reluctant but agree to have another vote if you can bring some evidence.

Quest 2: Gathering Evidence

  • Objective: Collect evidence to support the Seventh Dreamer's warning.
  • Description: Investigate sites of recent disturbances and gather evidence that corroborates the Seventh Dreamer's vision.
  • Reward: Evidence documents and a small amount of experience.

Quest 3: The Council's Trial

  • Objective: Present the evidence to the Council.
  • Description: Participate in a Council meeting where you present the gathered evidence and argue the need for immediate action.
  • Reward: Council's tentative approval and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 4: Uniting the Council

  • Objective: Resolve the conflicts among Council members.
  • Description: Mediate between the conflicting Council members to resolve their disputes and unify them towards a common goal.
  • Reward: Council’s unity and a small amount of experience.

Quest 5: The Ritual of Insight

  • Objective: Perform a ritual to gain deeper understanding.
  • Description: Assist the Council in performing a ritual that allows them to see a shared vision, confirming the Seventh Dreamer's warning.
  • Reward: Ritual completion and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 6: Thwarting the Enemy's Plan

  • Objective: Discover a way to prevent the enemy from fulfilling their goal.
  • Description: Investigate ancient texts, consult with wise sages, and decipher prophecies to find a method to thwart the enemy’s plans without engaging in full-scale war.
  • Reward: Key strategy to disrupt the enemy’s plan and a significant amount of experience.

Quest 7: The Relic of Time

  • Objective: Find a lost relic that allows the Dreamer to alter the past in their dreams.
  • Description: Investigate ancient ruins, consult with knowledgeable historians, and follow cryptic clues to locate a powerful relic that grants the ability to change past events within dreams. This relic could be the key to preventing the enemy's plan from coming to fruition.
  • Reward: The Relic of Time, significant experience boost, and a unique skill or ability to alter past events in dreams.

Chapter 5: Uniting the Dreamers - Quests

These quests for Chapter 5 focus on the council telling you about the old castle ruins. Hidden away in a valley of mists. They instruct you to go and see if you can find any clues to stop the [big bad guy]’s plans.

Quest 1: The Castle's Secret

  • Objective: Travel to the old castle ruins and investigate its secrets.
  • Description: Begin your journey to the ancient castle ruins, rumored to hold vital information about the original dreamer and their history.
  • Reward: Entry into the castle ruins and a small amount of experience.

Quest 2: Altering the Past

  • Objective: Use your new ability to alter the past within the castle ruins.
  • Description: Utilize the Relic of Time to change past events within the ruins, solving puzzles and bypassing obstacles that were previously insurmountable.
  • Reward: Access to deeper parts of the castle and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 3: The Guardian's Test

  • Objective: Overcome the castle's guardian by altering key moments in its past.
  • Description: Face the ancient guardian of the castle, using your ability to alter past events to weaken it and ultimately defeat it.
  • Reward: Defeated guardian, access to the castle's inner sanctum, and a small amount of experience.

Quest 4: The Hidden Library

  • Objective: Locate the hidden library within the castle ruins.
  • Description: Search the castle’s inner sanctum for a hidden library that contains valuable texts and records about the Dreamers.
  • Reward: Discovery of the hidden library and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 5: The Ancient Journal

  • Objective: Retrieve the old journal from the hidden library.
  • Description: Find and secure the ancient journal that contains crucial information about the original dreamer.
  • Reward: The ancient journal and a significant amount of experience.

Quest 6: Deciphering the Journal

  • Objective: Work with the Council to decipher the journal’s contents.
  • Description: Return to the Council with the journal and help decipher its contents to uncover the secrets it holds.
  • Reward: Vital information about the [big bad guy]’s weaknesses and a small amount of experience.

Quest 7: The Rule of Sevent

  • Objective: Use the journal’s information to gain a prophetic vision.
  • Description: Perform a ritual using the knowledge from the journal to trigger a powerful vision that reveals that only seven dreamers can exist at any time.
  • Reward: Major revelation, strategic insights, and a unique skill or item.
  • Story Point: You are a dreamer…the eighth. How is this possible?

Chapter 6: The Final Prophecy - Quests

These quests for Chapter 6 focus on uncovering the full extent of the impending threat, preparing for the climactic battle, and leveraging the combined prophetic abilities of the Dreamers to outmaneuver and defeat the enemy. Each quest builds up to the ultimate confrontation, ensuring that all preparations are in place and that the Council, and their allies are ready to face the challenge.

Quest 1: The Ominous Vision

  • Objective: Experience a powerful vision that reveals the enemy’s plans.
  • Description: Use your Dreamer abilities to delve into a profound vision that uncovers the details of the impending threat.
  • Reward: Detailed understanding of the enemy’s plans and a small amount of experience.
  • Story Point: You realize that one of the six council members is not who they claimed to be. But is the original dreamer.

Quest 2: Confronting [big bad guy]

  • Objective: Confront Selene (one of the council members), only to find her and the others missing.
  • Description: Go to confront one of the six Council members with your findings, but discover that they, along with the other five members, have mysteriously disappeared.
  • Reward: Clues about the Council's whereabouts and a small amount of experience.

Quest 2: The Enemy’s Whereabouts

  • Objective: Locate Selene and the missing council members.
  • Description: Find clues to locate Selene and the missing council members.
  • Reward: Access to new area and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 3: The Lost Allies

  • Objective: Rescue captured allies from the enemy’s clutches.
  • Description: Locate and liberate allies who have been captured by the enemy, gaining their assistance and knowledge.
  • Reward: Freed allies, additional support, and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 5: To Safety

  • Objective: Return the 5 council members back to town and ensure their safety.
  • Description: Guide the party back through the new area fending off monsters and attacks.
  • Reward: small amount of experience.

Quest 6: The Council’s Resolve

  • Objective: Hold a final strategy meeting with the Council.
  • Description: Convene with the Council to finalize the plan for taking on Selene.
  • Reward: Access to new area [Selene’s Forest Encampment] and a moderate amount of experience.

Quest 7: The Climactic Battle

  • Objective: Engage in the final battle against Selene.
  • Description: Travel to Selene’s Forest Encampment and confront her.
  • Reward: Victory over the enemy, significant experience boost, and a unique item or ability.
  • Story Point: You do not kill her, she escapes through time weakened and wounded. Will she return?
