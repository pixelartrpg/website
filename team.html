About Us

We are a small team. Most of the work is being done by my wife and I. My close friend Wromial is assisting with content development and being a sounding board for my crazy ideas. We all grew up playing 16 bit era video games with a special love for RPGs. We hope to bring a fun story, interesting mechanics, and lots of replayability to the indie dev space. Thank you for checking out our little site.

  • Dagor

    Developer\Writer

    I am currently a full time DevOps Engineer. I started my career as a web developer over 20 years ago, shifting over to developer with the grand idea of being a game developer. I think many of my fellow devs started with the same grandiose idea! Well, over the two decades of being a professional developer, db admin, and now devops engineer, I have worked on small personal project games here and there with nothing to show for it. As the indie dev scene seems to really be taking off with tools and resources, I decided I wanted to finally make a game I can call my own.

  • Larimaria

    Pixel Artist

    I grew up playing games like Chrono Trigger, Secret Of Mana, Final Fantasy, and Zelda. I am the definition of gamer girl and challenge anyone to mario cart! I have always been an artist at heart, taking summer art classes from elementary all the way through to college. I obtained my BFA in Sculpture with a major in jewelry. When my husband asked me to learn to draw pixel art I was excited and nervous all at once. Pixel art was a whole new medium I was not familiar with and I knew it was going to take some practice.
